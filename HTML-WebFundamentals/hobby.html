Digital Art: My Escape from Reality

Why I Love Digital Art

Digital art allows me to express my creativity without the limitations of traditional mediums. Ever since I was young, art has always been an interest of mine and I always found art material expensive. But due to my eagerness to be able to draw even with limitations, I found out about digital medium.

It gives me the stage to create my imaginations and live with them. I've always loved reading. I remembered back before the pandemic, I always read in Wattpad and other reading websites. I especially love reading Webtoons and Manga.

Softwares I use (PC and Mobile):

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Clip Studio Paint
  • Paint Tool SAI
  • Ibis Paint
  • Procreate
